About Us

Meihua Musical Instruments

Meihua Musical Instruments is a professional musical instrument factory located in Zheng'an County, Guizhou Province, the world's largest musical instrument production base. With 18 years of OEM manufacturing experience, we focus on acoustic guitars, electric guitars and ukuleles for overseas distributors, brand owners, online sellers, education suppliers and retail channels.

Our factory supports OEM and ODM service for body shapes, wood species, hardware finishes, pickups, electronics, custom packaging and private-label requirements. Buyers can start from an existing model, a reference sample, a photo, a drawing or a specification sheet.

For every new project, our sales and production team reviews the target market, expected quantity, target price, package requirement and delivery schedule before confirming a workable sample plan.

OEM / ODM Service

Customization Process from Brief to Repeat Order

1. Product Brief Share body shape, target market, specs, quantity and price range.
2. Solution Review Confirm wood species, hardware finishes, pickups, electronics, packaging, cost target and sample direction.
3. Sample Build Develop sample guitars or ukuleles with selected materials, logo and configuration.
4. Bulk Production Arrange material preparation, production schedule and in-process quality checks.
5. Final QC Inspect appearance, neck fit, fretwork, electronics, setup and packing details.
6. Shipment Support Prepare export packing, commercial documents and follow-up support for repeat orders.

Quality Control

Inspection Points Before Shipment

Material CheckWood stability, moisture condition, visible defects and key component matching are reviewed before production.
Structure CheckNeck joint, bridge position, body assembly, fretwork and hardware installation are checked during production.
Playability SetupString height, intonation, fret buzz, neck relief and tuning stability are adjusted before packing.
Electronics TestElectric guitars and pickup-equipped acoustic guitars are tested for output, noise, switching and control response.
Appearance ReviewFinish, color, logo placement, binding, rosette, scratches and visible surface details are inspected.
Packing CheckAccessories, labels, cartons, protection material and shipping marks are checked according to buyer requirements.
